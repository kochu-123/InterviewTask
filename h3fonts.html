Generic Font Families

Five generic font families :-

Serif :- Have a small stroke at the edge of each letter.Create a sence of formality and elegance.
Sans-Serif :- Have clean lines. Create a modern minimalistic look.
Monospace :- All the letters have the same fixed width.Create a mechanical look.
Cursive :- Imitate human handwriting.
Fantasy :- Decorative/playful fonts.

Web Safe Fonts

Fonts that are universally installed across all browsers & devices.

Fallback Fonts

There are no 100% completely web safe fonts. There is always a chance that a
font is not found or is not installed properly.
Therefore,it is important to always use fallback fonts.
